Personality Prediction Model

Our Mission

To streamline the process of the Meyers-Briggs personality identification based off of the least amount of information possible with a 70% or higher accuracy rate.

Why?

The Meyers-Briggs test is comprised of approximately 70 questions. Aint nobody got time for that!

How is this beneficial?

The potential uses are vast. Employers can use this test to identify the personality types of their best performing associates and then leverage this model when selecting potential candidates for hiring. A person could use this to detemrine their significant other's personality type.

How does it work?

Magic, but don't tell anyone. It is a limited resource.
